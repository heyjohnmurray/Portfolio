Design Considerations

Finding unique imagery is one of the hardest parts of designing for multiple partners in the same industry. So many insurance companies use photos of happy families or young couples loading up the car for a road trip. As an attempt to break away from the expected, I decided to use imagery of the open road. I hoped it would convey a sense of freedom and independence to the customer. I wanted the consumer to subconsciously feel that that freedom could be gained by getting auto coverage through MetLife.
